remember

A membrane-based Plone member implementation.

This product is NOT yet ready for use.  It doesn't work yet.  There is
NO support at this time.  Please do not ask for assistance with this
product unless you are an accomplished Plone developer and you are
planning on spending a significant amount of time helping to develop
this product.

============
REQUIREMENTS
============

- Zope 2.9.3
- Plone 2.5
- membrane  (http://svn.plone.org/svn/collective/membrane/trunk)
- py-bcrypt (http://www.mindrot.org/py-bcrypt.html or 
             http://cheeseshop.python.org/pypi/bcrypt/0.1)
